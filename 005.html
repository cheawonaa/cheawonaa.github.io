Dear Nature (2019)

Project by doing field research of Het Nationale Park De Hoge Veluwe, Buitenplaats, Arnhem. We felt so sorry to this forest and wanted to show new scenery to the forest. We did performance as a ceremony to comfort this forest by using colors that don't exist in nature and choreography that opposite to army movements.

Collaboration with Jinsun Bae, Carmen Prium
