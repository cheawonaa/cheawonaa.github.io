I live here (2019)

Inspired by the book 'Invisible Cities' by Italo Calvino.
There is a short story about Lares who has a bad temper and original dweller in the city. I imagine Lares as a cloud of dust because the book says they live under the cupboard, table, and everywhere. Lares had to deal with new people in their area. Their story reminds me of city policies in real world. City can be redesigned again and dwellers have to move because of the rising housing fee. A number of people move in and the city becomes crowded. This short film shows that in an allegory story.
