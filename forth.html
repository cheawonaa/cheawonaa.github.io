Brought to you by the letter 'i'


Using the article 'Brought To You By the Letter i' from Jessica Winter, I made a short book out of it.
The story treats letters as a human and portrait their story like an allegory. I really like that author treats them as living creatures. They are not only letters but also shapes. So I treated page numbers as a shape and took them to pieces when their duty is done. Every time a page passes, those page number shapes continue to pile up.
