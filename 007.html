Redesign of OASE #79 (2019)

OASE #79 talks mainly about the architect, James Stirling. He's interview shows how he was inspired and how he applied it to his work. I wanted to put a dreamy feeling with the format of a book. And wanted to make readers read it with appreciation. The yellow one is about James Stirling's imaginative idea part and Blue one is about how he translates those ideas into reality.
