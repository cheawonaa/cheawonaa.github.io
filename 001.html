Objects for AI (2020)

AI and robots are getting smarter and smarter.
They can visualize and digitize almost every sense which humans have. However, the sense of touch seems tricky to learn for them. We human beings can use seeing and feeling simultaneous that AI can't. I wanted to interrupt AI's learning process with my own objects that its property and visual don't match. After that, I used an AI app to react to my objects and documented them in the format of the book. This work shows how AI is still far from imitating the ability of human beings which is our accumulated visual dataset of textures and our natural talent for tactile.
