Objects for AI (2020)

AI and robot are getting smarter and smarter. They can visualize and digitize almost every sense which humans have. However, the sense of touch seems tricky one for them. We human beings can use seeing and feeling simultaneously but AI can't. I wanted to interrupt AI's learning process with my own objects. These object's property and visual doesn't match. I used AI app to react on my objects and documented in a format of the book. This work shows the greatness of our accumulated visual dataset of textures and our natural talant of feeling. for the whole book, click here!
